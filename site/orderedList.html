Garlic Sauce Recipe

  1. Peel the garlic
  2. Chop the peeled garlic
  3. Break 2 eggs and put them in the blender
  4. Squeeze half a lemon
  5. Add to the eggs in the blender:
    1. The chopped garlic
    2. The extract of lemon juice
    3. A pinch of Salt and Pepper
    4. 5 drops of liquid stevia
  6. Start whipping the mixture
  7. Add a fine thread of olive oil while still whipping
  8. Just continue the last step until the mixture take some consistency
